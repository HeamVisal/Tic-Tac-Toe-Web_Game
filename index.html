How to play

Tic-tac-toe (also called noughts and crosses) is a classic strategy game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking a square on the grid. The first player to line up enough marks in a row — horizontally, vertically or diagonally — wins the round.

Pick your challenge: the classic 3×3 board needs three in a row, 5×5 needs four, and the big 7×7 and 10×10 boards need five in a row. Play against a friend on the same device, invite an online friend with a shared link, or face the computer on easy, tricky or perfect difficulty. Scores are saved in your browser between visits. Free to play, no ads, no sign-up.
